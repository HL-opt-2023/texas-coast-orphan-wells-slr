Sea-level rise risk

Primary modality: inundation. Once an orphan wellhead submerges, casing/cement corrosion accelerates in saltwater, inspection access is lost, and any leak releases directly into open water. This module queries NOAA Office for Coastal Management's published inundation polygons (1–10 ft above current Mean Higher High Water) and, for each ranked orphan well, predicts the lowest SLR scenario at which it becomes inundated.
